About Spot

At Spot, we’re dedicated to transforming urban parking into a seamless, stress-free experience. Born from our team’s firsthand frustrations with city parking, our mission is to eliminate the endless search for available, affordable parking. By providing real-time access to nearby parking lots and garages, we empower commuters, students, event-goers, and city explorers to find the right spot—at the right price—without the hassle. With Spot, navigating city life becomes easier, one parking space at a time.
